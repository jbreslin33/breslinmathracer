How can Heidi find the number of pencils that can be made in 6 hours?
 
 A.
Heidi can add 7 + 6 = 13. Then she can add two zeroes to the end of the number because 700 is a multiple of 100. The answer is 1,300 pencils.
 B.
Heidi can multiply 7 6 = 42. Then she can add one zero to the end of the number because 700 is a multiple of 10. The answer is 420 pencils.
C.
Heidi can multiply 7 6 = 42. Then she can add two zeroes to the end of the number because 700 is a multiple of 100. The answer is 4,200 pencils.
 D.
Heidi can add 7 + 6 = 13. Then she can add one zero to the end of the number because 700 is a multiple of 10. The answer is 130 pencils.
