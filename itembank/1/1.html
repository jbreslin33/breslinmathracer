Item 25: Name/ID: Geometry 1.G.3 / 1.G.3
Proctor Instructions: Make sure all students are ready for the question.
 
Say: Look at the two shapes below.
 

 
Say: Which of the following is true about the shapes?
 
Say: Because shape A and shape B are equal, the parts are equal; because shape A is divided into two equal parts, the parts are smaller; because shape B is divided into four parts, the parts are smaller; or because there are the same number of parts in shape A and shape B, the parts are equal? Choose the correct answer.
 
 A.
Because shape A and shape B are equal, the parts are equal.
 B.
Because shape A is divided into two equal parts, the parts are smaller.
C.
Because shape B is divided into four parts, the parts are smaller.
 D.
Because there are the same number of parts in shape A and shape B, the parts are equal.
