Item 26: Name/ID: Geometry 1.G.3 / 1.G.3
Proctor Instructions: Make sure all students are ready for the question.
 
Say: Mary made a flower. She used a circle for the top of the flower and divided it in half.
 
Say: Draw a flower that could be Mary's. Then draw a different figure that uses a circle divided into four equal shares.

Item 28: Name/ID: Geometry 1.G.3 / 1.G.3
Proctor Instructions: Make sure all students are ready for the question.
 
Say: Sal is drawing a picture of a park. He starts with a rectangular piece of paper and divides it so that the park will have four equal sections.
 
Say: Draw a picture of the park Sal could have drawn.
 
Say: Describe each part of the park, and describe the whole park in terms of how the park is divided into parts.
