Item 162: Name/ID: Number and Operations in Base Ten 2.NBT.9 / 2.NBT.9
Diego was asked to solve the problem 49 + 32.
 
He used the base-10 blocks below to find the sum.
 
 
Which gives the correct answer and explains how to use the base-10 blocks to find the sum?
 
 A.
Make a pile of 49 and a pile of 32. Together, that is 7 tens and 11 ones. Since 11 ones is the same as 1 ten with 1 extra, that makes 7 tens and 11 ones. So, the answer is 711.
B.
Make a pile of 49 and a pile of 32. Together, that is 7 tens and 11 ones. Since 11 ones is the same as 1 ten with 1 extra, that makes 8 tens and 1 one. So, the answer is 81.
 C.
Make a pile of 49 and a pile of 32. Together, that is 7 tens and 11 ones. Since 11 ones is the same as 1 ten with 1 extra, that makes 7 tens and 1 one. So, the answer is 71.
 D.
Make a pile of 49 and a pile of 32. Together, that is 7 tens and 11 ones. Since 11 ones is the same as 1 ten with 1 extra, and 1 + 1 is 2 that makes 9 tens and 1 one. So the answer is 91.
