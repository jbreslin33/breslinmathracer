Item 60: Name/ID: Measurement and Data 3.MD.7.c / 3.MD.7.c

 A.
Multiply both sections of the area model, and subtract the products.
6 x 7 = 42
6 x 2 = 12
42 - 12 = 30
 
 B.
 
Multiply the side lengths.
6 x 5 = 5 x 6
 
C.
 
Multiply both sections of the area model, and add the products.
6 x 5 = 30
6 x 4 = 24
30 + 24 = 54
 D.
 
Multiply each section of the area model.
6 x 2 x 2 x 6 = 144
