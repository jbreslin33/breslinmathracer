Item 127: Name/ID: Number and Operations in Base Ten 3.NBT.2 / 3.NBT.2
Elaine dropped some straws. She picked up 126 straws, and her friend picked up 225. Elaine used mental math to figure out how many straws she dropped. Which answer correctly shows Elaine's thinking process?
 
 A.
Elaine knows 100 + 200 is 300. She also knows, 25 + 25 is 50. Subtract 1 for the 26. So, 300 + 50 - 1 is 349. She dropped 349 straws. 
B.
Elaine knows 100 + 200 is 300. She also knows, 25 + 25 is 50. Add 1 more for the 26. So, 300 + 50 + 1 is 351. She dropped 351 straws. 
 C.
Elaine knows 100 + 200 is 300. She also knows, 25 + 25 is 50. Add 1 more for the 26. So, 300 + 60 is 360. She dropped 360 straws. 
 D.
Elaine knows 25 + 25 is 50. She also knows, 100 + 200 is 300. Add the 1 carried from the 26 to equal 400. So, 400 + 50 is 450. She dropped 450 straws.
