Item 228: Name/ID: Operations and Algebraic Thinking 4.OA.2 / 4.OA.2
Claire reads the story problem below.
 
A medium pizza costs 12 times as much as one slice of pizza. If one slice of pizza costs $0.50, how much does a medium pizza cost?
 
 
Which of the following explains whether Claire is correct?
 
 A.
Claire is incorrect. She found the sum of 12 and 0.50 when she should have found the quotient of 12 and 0.50. The cost of a medium pizza is $24.00.
B.
Claire is incorrect. She found the sum of 12 and 0.50 when she should have found the product of 0.50 and 12. The cost of a medium pizza is $6.00.
 C.
Claire is incorrect. She found the sum of 12 and 0.50 when she should have subtracted 0.50 from 12. The cost of a medium pizza is $11.50.
 D.
Claire is correct. To find the price of a medium pizza, find the sum of 12 and 0.50. The cost of a medium pizza is $12.50.
